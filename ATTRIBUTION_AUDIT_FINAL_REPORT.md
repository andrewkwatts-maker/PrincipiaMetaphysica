## METHODOLOGY NOTES

### Attribution Philosophy
PM framework follows academic standard of complete attribution:
1. **Original discoverers** cited with dates
2. **Key papers** with DOI/arXiv when available
3. **Standard textbooks** for pedagogical references
4. **Modern reviews** for comprehensive overviews

### "Used in PM" Sections
Each attribution includes:
- **Specific usage:** How PM employs the concept
- **Theory sections:** Links to where concept appears in PM framework
- **Examples:** Concrete instances (e.g., "M_H = 125.25 GeV → Re(T) = 8.43")

Following PM's philosophical commitment (philosophical-implications.html), attributions distinguish:
- **Topologically derived** (e.g., 3 generations from G₂ topology)
- **Dynamically emergent** (e.g., gauge couplings from RG flow)
- **Observationally constrained** (e.g., Re(T) from measured M_H)
