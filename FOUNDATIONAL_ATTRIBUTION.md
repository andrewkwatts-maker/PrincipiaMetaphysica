## 5. Style Guidelines Compliance

### Academic Citation Format:
✅ **Consistent format:** (Author, Year) used throughout inline citations
✅ **Respectful language:** "builds upon", "following", "extends"
✅ **No diminishing language:** Avoided "improves on" or "fixes"
✅ **Acknowledgment of debts:** Explicit statements like "We owe a profound debt..."

### Examples of Respectful Attribution Language:

**From Paper Theoretical Foundations:**
> "This work builds upon the foundational contributions of numerous pioneering theorists whose insights have shaped our understanding of fundamental physics and mathematics. We owe a profound debt to their groundbreaking research, upon which this framework stands."

**From Index Footer:**
> "This framework stands on the shoulders of giants. We are deeply indebted to the pioneering theorists whose foundational contributions have shaped modern physics and mathematics."

**From geometric-framework.html:**
> "The 7-dimensional G₂ manifold (following Bryant's construction [Bryant, 1987] and Joyce's classification [Joyce, 2000])"

**From fermion-sector.html:**
> "The Clifford algebra Cl(p,q) (Clifford, 1878) is generated by Γ-matrices (generalizing Dirac's 4D gamma matrices [Dirac, 1928])"
