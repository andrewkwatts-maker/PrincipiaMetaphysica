4. Study Polchinski Vol. 1 (Chapters 1-3)

1. Review Standard Model basics (Griffiths or Peskin & Schroeder Ch. 1-14)
2. Study Aitchison & Hey "Gauge Theories" (Chapters 15-17)
3. Read Langacker's GUT review (arXiv:0901.0241)
4. Explore SO(10) phenomenology papers

**Path 3: Neutrino Physics**
1. Watch Fermilab neutrino videos
2. Read NuFIT website for latest experimental results
3. Study Giunti & Kim textbook (Chapters 6-8)
4. Read André de Gouvêa TASI lectures (arXiv:hep-ph/0411274)

**Path 4: Differential Geometry for G₂**
1. Review basic Riemannian geometry (do Carmo or Lee)
2. Study holonomy groups (Besse or Salamon)
3. Read Karigiannis intro to G₂ (arXiv:0807.3858)
4. Explore Joyce's book for advanced topics

**Path 5: Cosmology & Dark Energy**
1. Start with Ryden "Introduction to Cosmology"
2. Read Dodelson & Schmidt "Modern Cosmology" (Chapters 6-8)
3. Study dark energy reviews (arXiv:hep-th/0603057)
4. Check DESI collaboration results
