## Glossary of Key Concepts

### For Non-Specialists

- **GUT (Grand Unified Theory):** Framework unifying electromagnetic, weak, and strong forces at high energy (~10¹⁶ GeV)
- **Proton Decay:** Predicted decay of protons in GUTs (e.g., p → e⁺π⁰) with lifetime ~10³⁴ years
- **KK (Kaluza-Klein) Modes:** Tower of massive particles arising from compactified extra dimensions
- **Higgs Mechanism:** Process giving mass to particles via spontaneous symmetry breaking
- **PMNS Matrix:** Neutrino mixing matrix (analogous to CKM for quarks)
- **Dark Energy EoS:** Equation of state w = P/ρ describing dark energy pressure vs density
- **Compactification:** Hiding extra dimensions by making them small and compact
- **Thermal Time:** Emergent time parameter from thermodynamic entropy (Connes-Rovelli)
- **G₂ Manifold:** 7D space with exceptional holonomy group G₂ (used in M-theory)
