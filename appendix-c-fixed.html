Z₂ Mirror Structure and Dark Sector

The Z₂ orbifold action in the 26D framework relates gauge sectors across the temporal mirror:

$$\text{Observable } SO(10) \leftrightarrow \text{Mirror } SO(10)$$

  • Both sectors undergo identical breaking chains: \(SO(10) \to SU(3) \times SU(2) \times U(1)\)
  • Mirror sector gauge bosons do not couple directly to our matter
  • Interaction occurs only gravitationally (exchange of KK modes, mirror gravitons)
  • Provides natural dark matter candidates without ad-hoc hidden sector
  • Z₂ symmetry ensures identical mass spectra in both sectors (mirror nucleons, atoms, etc.)
