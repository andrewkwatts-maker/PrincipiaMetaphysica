1. ADMX (Axion Dark Matter eXperiment) -- Haloscope
------------------------------------------------------
Status: Leading search for cold DM QCD axions in classic window (~2-40 microeV).

Current exclusion limits (ADMX Phase II & III, 2024-2025):
  Mass range excluded (90% CL): 2.66-3.31 microeV and 3.95-4.55 microeV
  Coupling excluded: g_{a gamma gamma} ~ 10^{-15}-10^{-16} GeV^{-1} in those ranges (KSVZ model)

Projected sensitivity (ADMX Phase III & IV, 2025-2028):
  Expected to cover ~2-40 microeV (f_a ~ 0.3-6e12 GeV)
  Sensitivity to g_{a gamma gamma} down to ~10^{-16} GeV^{-1}
  Full coverage of classic QCD axion window expected by ~2028-2030

Theory prediction vs ADMX:
  Predicted mass: ~6.3 microeV -- just outside currently excluded regions
  Predicted coupling: ~1.71e-12 GeV^{-1} -- above current sensitivity, within Phase III/IV reach
  Status: NOT excluded. In high-priority target region for upcoming runs.

Haloscope principle:
  Resonant conversion a -> gamma in strong magnetic field (Primakoff effect):
  g_{a gamma gamma} B^2 L^2 Q ~ signal power
  B ~ 8.5 T, Q ~ 10^5-10^6, Frequency: 0.65-2.0 GHz -> m_a ~ 2.7-8.3 microeV
