======================================================
Source: TwoLayerOR And Dark Forces.txt

PROPOSAL SUMMARY:
Dark force leakage across shadows is highly asymmetric. General formula for any gauge
boson of group G:

GENERAL FORMULA:
A_leak,G = R_n · exp(-S_E) · C_G
P_leak,G = |R_n|² · exp(-2S_E) · |C_G|²
α_leak,G = √P_leak,G

Common prefactor: P_base = (1/12)² · e^{-12} ≈ 6.9 × 10^{-6}

GROUP-SPECIFIC SUPPRESSION FACTORS:

1. STRONG FORCE (SU(3)_C gluons):
   - Confinement → color-neutral instanton required
   - S_inst ~ 8π²/g_s² ≈ 80 (at GUT scale)
   - C_G,strong ≈ e^{-80} ~ 10^{-35}
   - P_leak,strong ~ 10^{-75}
   - α_leak,strong ~ 10^{-37.5} → EFFECTIVELY ZERO

2. WEAK FORCE (SU(2)_L W/Z):
   - Massive bosons (m_W ~ 80 GeV) → tunneling suppressed
   - C_G,weak ≈ e^{-m_W r_bridge} ~ e^{-10^5} ~ 10^{-43400}
   - P_leak,weak ~ 0 → EFFECTIVELY ZERO

3. ELECTROMAGNETIC (U(1)_Y photons):
   - Massless, parity-even → moderate suppression
   - C_G,EM ≈ 1 (no gauge suppression)
   - α_leak,light ≈ 0.00248 (~230× weaker than DM portal)

4. GRAVITY (gravitons):
   - Massless, universal → weakest suppression
   - C_G,grav ≈ 1 (gravitons couple to everything)
   - P_leak,grav ~ 6.9×10^{-6}
   - α_leak,grav ≈ 0.00248 (same as photons, but universal)
