THE ERROR IDENTIFIED

LOCATION: Appendix E.4, Line 1884 of principia-metaphysica-paper.html

WHAT'S WRITTEN:
  M_GUT = M_Pl × exp(-κ × 2π / g_GUT²)
        = M_Pl × exp(-1.46 × 2π / 0.0415)
        = 2.12 × 10^16 GeV

THE BUG:
  The formula symbol says "g_GUT²" but the number is 0.0415

  0.0415 is NOT g_GUT²
  0.0415 IS α_GUT (the fine structure constant)

  The correct g_GUT² value is:
    g_GUT² = 4π × α_GUT = 4π × (1/24.10) = 0.521426
