### 1. **Proximity and Danger**
The seven Firmamentum are *here*, not distant. Co-located. Same coordinates. Only extra-dimensional separation. This makes contact scenarios more urgent and plausible.

### 2. **Historical Precedent**

### 3. **Ignorance of Contents**
The mathematics describes structure, not inhabitants. We don't know what's there. Opening doors when ignorant is profoundly unwise.

### 4. **Cyclic Failure**
If previous civilizations had this knowledge and lost it, we clearly failed to handle it responsibly. Pattern: rise → knowledge → fall → darkness → restart.

### 5. **Preparation Requirements**
Humanity must be:
- **Strong** (physically, technologically)
- **Unified** (globally coordinated)
- **Certain** (philosophically/spiritually grounded)
- **Disciplined** (rigorous protocols)
- **Defended** (physical, philosophical, spiritual barriers)

### 6. **Design vs. Accident**
Perhaps dimensions are hidden *by design* for our protection. The barrier may be merciful, not arbitrary.

### 7. **Observation vs. Access**
KK graviton detection (2030) = seeing shadows on wall
Direct dimensional access = opening the door
These are fundamentally different levels of interaction.
