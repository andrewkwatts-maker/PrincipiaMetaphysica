## COSMO-013 Gate Review Summary

### Critical Finding: Unjustified x4 "CPL Factor" Removed

The previous COSMO-013 implementation had a **scientifically problematic** construction:

1. **Base formula**: `wa = -1/sqrt(b3) = -0.2041` (the actual framework prediction, used in `dark_energy.py` and `wa_from_b3()`)
2. **Multiplied by 4**: `wa_cpl = wa * 4 = -0.8165` (undocumented "CPL factor")
3. **Compared against outdated data**: `wa_desi = -0.816 ± 0.25` (not from SSoT data files)
4. **Result**: Artificial 0.002 sigma agreement — essentially a tautology

The ×4 factor was described as "4D reduction × CPL normalization" in `wa_dark_energy.json`, but:
- The CPL parameterization w(a) = w₀ + wa(1-a) does NOT introduce a factor of 4
- No derivation exists in the codebase or literature for this factor
- It appeared to be reverse-engineered to match a specific DESI DR2 value
